Want to Know About Us?

Usha Solar Power offers a smart sustainable energy solutions ranging from installing individual solar panels to complete PV systems. We are continuously shaping development of solar energy as a source of regenerative energy.

Usha Solar Power is a complete solar energy system provider, offering everything from consultancy to maintenance under one roof. We are one of the pioneers in the field of solar technology and global promoters of renewable smart energy generated from sunlight. Usha solar delivers maximum value and superb performance throughout the long life of every solar systems. We are yours perfect companion, If you want to take one responsible step closer to the nature by switching to renewable energies and at the same time by saving noticeably on your energy costs.

Our Mission

We, at Usha Solar Power are committed to leading a change in a country’s current energy streams by providing cleaner and smarter energy choices, thereby going with the sole mission to deescalate India’s dependence on limited conventional energy sources.
Being a responsible leader in providing clean energy solutions our Philosophy starts-and-ends at Client-first approach. Be it understanding your business requirements to choosing the right technologies, we work to ensure you to provide the ideal energy solutions at minimum expenses. We always offer free and customized solar assessments according to your needs to accelerate the adoption of solar technology across the world to conserve our environment and provide a sustainable and conflict-free power supply.
